Who Are We.

Mechismu Racing believes in Action, Progress & Growth. Our Team has achieved several milestones in its active years till now but nothing holds us back from seeking for more. As a team we roll up our sleeves to plant roots in the Formula communities that we call home. Since its formation in 2008, our team has constantly been developing and deploying innovative formula car models, pushing the boundaries of the Indian automobiles. After dominating the IC Engine domain, we’ve embarked on a journey to build modern and advanced systems of electric cars with some of the most remarkable and enthusiastic formula aspirants of IIT ISM Dhanbad. Our passion is what drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential because after all Racing is Religion!

What We Do.

We design, engineer, and race our cars as a racing team in SAE Collegiate Design Competitions such as Formula SAE, Baja SAE, and Supra SAE where students are challenged to build and race miniature formula-style racing cars. We rely significantly on innovation and experimentation in our quest towards greatness. We plan, build, and prepare the vehicle for the competition, which includes both static and dynamic events.

What is FSAE.

Formula Student (FS) is one of the most prestigious educational engineering competitions hosted by the Formula Student community. Spread across 5 continents, Formula Student captivates a large number of universities every year to participate and compete with each other.

Our Goal.

Passion, respect, innovation,and determination and excellence are the values which guide us towards our goal.Past performance is not an necessarily indicator of future success, we are aware of our competitors and we aspire for more speed.We plan,build and prepare our vehicles for static and dynamic events.

Our Mission.

Being aspiring engineers and racing fanatics ,we plan and build formula prototype race cars, which participates into national and international formula Student competitions
